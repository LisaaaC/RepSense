# RepSense

---

## Overview

RepSense AI is an intelligent fitness agent that provides real-time coaching by combining computer vision, personalised nutrition planning, and evidence-based supplement guidance.

It aims to make expert-level fitness support accessible to anyone, anywhere, without the cost or limitations of personal trainers.

---

## Problem

Millions of people train without proper guidance. As a result, many:

* Use incorrect exercise form, increasing the risk of injury
* Follow generic diet plans that do not reflect their cultural preferences
* Take supplements without understanding whether they are necessary
* Lack access to affordable, consistent coaching

---

## Solution

RepSense AI addresses these challenges by bringing together three core capabilities:

Movement Intelligence

* Real-time exercise form analysis using computer vision
* Automatic rep counting and technique scoring
* Detection of movement patterns that may lead to injury

Nutrition Planning

* Personalised meal plans based on user goals and body metrics
* Support for culturally relevant diets, including halal, vegan, Hindu vegetarian, and high-protein diets

Supplement Guidance

* Evidence-based recommendations
* Aligned with individual training goals and needs

---

## How It Works

RepSense AI is built on four key components:

Computer Vision Engine
Detects posture, joint alignment, and movement patterns during exercise

AI Performance Agent
Processes user data to generate real-time coaching insights

Nutrition and Supplement Module
Calculates nutritional requirements and generates personalised plans

User Interface
Allows users to record exercises, input preferences, and interact with the AI in real time
